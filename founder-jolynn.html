Background

Two decades scaling healthcare businesses

JoLynn Smith-Joyner has spent more than 20 years across sales, operations, market entry, payer/provider strategy, and business transformation. She has worked inside and alongside organizations including UnitedHealthcare, Optum, large U.S. hospital networks, and medical device companies — helping scale teams, strengthen service delivery, accelerate revenue, and support two market exits.

At ArcCentrx, JoLynn leads operations, service delivery, training, people, and sales execution. Her work focuses on stabilizing operations, aligning teams, building scalable workflows, accelerating growth, and helping companies move toward stronger enterprise value and exit readiness.
